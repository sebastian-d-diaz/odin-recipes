Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

Grilled Cheese is a simple dish created by adding a slice of cheese in between two slices of white bread, and then cooking it in a pan, leaving you with cheesy, buttery goodness.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • American Cheese (Or whatever you like!)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add butter to both pieces of toast.
  2. On the side without butter, add your cheese and place the pieces of bread together
  3. Place on hot stove, and cook until bread is golden brown and cheese is all melted.
  4. Enjoy!
